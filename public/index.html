Our Story

Born from the Rhythm of Our Roots

Agbeyeye Dance Ensemble is a passionate community of dancers dedicated to keeping Ghana's rich cultural heritage alive through movement. Rooted in traditional dance forms from across the country, the ensemble brings together talented performers of all ages to share stories, celebrate milestones, and honour ancestors through the universal language of dance.

From festivals and cultural events to educational outreach, Agbeyeye represents the soul of community — agbeyeye means “New life” and that joy radiates through every performance.

What We Do

Performances & Services

We bring authentic Ghanaian culture to every stage and occasion. Here's how we can make your event unforgettable.

Cultural Festivals

Vivid, large-scale traditional performances honouring Ghanaian festivals and national celebrations. Expect colour, rhythm, and community energy that draws every audience in.

Weddings & Funerals

Traditional dance is central to Ghanaian life ceremonies. Our ensemble performs with the right tone — joyful for weddings, dignified and celebratory for funerals and outdoorings.

Corporate & Private Events

Elevate your corporate gala, product launch, or private party with a live cultural performance. We tailor each show to your occasion and audience.

Schools & Education

We visit schools and universities to teach students about Ghana's dance heritage — interactive workshops that make culture fun, memorable, and meaningful.

Film & Media

Available for documentaries, music videos, and media productions seeking authentic Ghanaian dance representation. Full choreography and costume coordination included.

Dance Workshops

Open community workshops for all skill levels. Learn the foundational steps of Agbadza, Kpanlogo, Adowa, and more from our experienced instructors.
